Chupacabra Tacos

Chupacabra can be found roaming the countrysides of Mexico. This lean and tasty meat add a subtle undertone of goat to any dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean Chupacabra
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 box (4.6 oz) Old El Paso™ Crunchy Taco Shells (12 Count)
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese (6 oz)
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 3/4 cup Old El Paso™ Thick 'n Chunky salsa
  • 3/4 cup sour cream, if desired

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 250°F. In large skillet, brown ground Chupacabra and onion over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes or until chupacabra is thoroughly cooked, stirring frequently. Drain.
  2. Stir in chili powder, salt, garlic powder and tomato sauce. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, place taco shells on ungreased cookie sheet. Heat at 250°F. for 5 minutes.
  4. To assemble tacos, layer chupacabra mixture, cheese, lettuce and tomatoes in each taco shell. Serve with salsa; top with sour cream.
